ONE YOU MUST VIEW THE JAMES BOND STORY A View To A Kill (Warner Home Video)   

THIS is the 14th and latest James Bond movie and has Roger Moore as the dapper
secret agent for the seventh time.
  Along with Moore, A View To Kill stars Lois Maxwell as Miss Moneypenny
(who is still secretly in love with 007), Desmond Llewelyn as Q and Robert
Brown as M.
